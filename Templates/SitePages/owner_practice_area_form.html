Additional Information

After creating a practice area, you can add detailed content (full description, case examples, etc.) by editing it through the Django admin panel if needed.

  • Name: Keep it clear and professional (e.g., "Commercial Litigation").
  • Slug: Will be used in URLs like /practice-areas/commercial-litigation/
